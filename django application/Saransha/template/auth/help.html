Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ResearchRadar

To upload your publication data, go to the "Upload" section and follow the instructions provided on the page. You can upload your data in various formats, including Excel, BibTeX, or CSV. Make sure your file contains a 'Profile URL' column with valid Google Scholar profile links.

Once you've uploaded your publication data, navigate to the "Generate Summary" section. You can customize your summary by filtering by publication type, year range, and other criteria. The generated summary will provide you with key metrics and a detailed list of your publications.

We support Excel files (.xlsx, .xls) and BibTeX files (.bib). Your Excel file must contain a 'Profile URL' column with valid Google Scholar profile links for the system to process your publications.

Processing time depends on the number of profiles in your file. Typically, processing takes a few minutes per profile. We process a maximum of 3 profiles per upload to ensure optimal performance. Please be patient during the processing.
